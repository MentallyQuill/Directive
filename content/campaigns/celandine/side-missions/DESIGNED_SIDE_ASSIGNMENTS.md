## Bees at Night

**ID:** `side-bees-at-night`  
**Kind:** side  
**Typical duration:** 1 session  
**Calm content:** Yes  
**Delegation:** Allowed

### Player-facing premise

Imported pollinators lose navigation under Weather Crown pulses, threatening orchard and seed plots without sabotage or conspiracy.

### Director frame

Keep this primarily scientific, operational, and cultural. It can generate an asset without becoming a hidden-villain story.

**Dramatic question:** Can the crew restore a living agricultural relationship that no amount of cargo can replace?

### Anchors

- Locations: `weather-crown`, `khepri`, `cardassian-orchard`
- Actors: `sovek`, `nomi-keth`, `toran-vel`
- Factions: `vela-wetland-stewardship`, `cardassian-reconstruction-mission`

### Objectives

1. Determine how weather telemetry disrupts pollinator orientation.
2. Protect colonies through a night flight and field intervention.
3. Leave local maintainers with a durable monitoring practice.

### Active pressures

- A weather correction for pollinators conflicts with one crop zone.
- Colonies cannot be replicated quickly.
- Local beekeepers distrust orbital control of the solution.

### Revelations and resilient routes

- **Core:** Pollinators use a magnetic cue distorted by one Weather Crown timing cycle. Alternate causal routes are permitted; do not require one scene or check.
- **Supporting:** Keth can map emotional crowd pressure but not insect behavior; local keepers remain essential. Alternate causal routes are permitted; do not require one scene or check.

### Example approaches

These are examples, not a menu of valid solutions.

- Retune the pulse schedule.
- Install local magnetic reference beacons.
- Move colonies temporarily and reseed habitat corridors.

### Authored outcome families

#### `pollinator-network-restored`

Pollinator routes and local maintenance are restored without regional security escalation.

- Adjust `ecological-health` by +2.
- Adjust `alternative-readiness` by +1.
- Grant asset `asset.pollinator-network`: Restored colonies and local keepers improve yields, crop diversity, and ecological observation.

#### `temporary-pollination-bridge`

Shuttle-assisted relocation protects the immediate bloom while local restoration continues.

- Adjust `ecological-health` by +1.

#### `bloom-missed`

- Adjust `famine-pressure` by +1.
