# Food Council and Seed Law Reference

## Legal baseline

The Cyradon Food Council is an emergency coordination body formed during the Dominion War. It schedules interworld shipments, publishes reserve forecasts, mediates allocation, and recognizes local harvest authorities. It is not a sovereign government, but wartime decrees gave it powers that many officials now treat as permanent.

## Seed categories

- **Emergency production stock:** seed or tissue authorized for immediate food security under temporary controls.
- **Heritage stock:** locally maintained lineages with cultural, ecological, and ownership claims.
- **Experimental substitute stock:** Grayleaf or other trial material subject to consent, adverse-result reporting, and restricted multiplication.
- **K-17 production lines:** current Dominion-engineered crops with strain-specific monitoring and control risk.
- **Parent strains and control materials:** Briar-origin material requiring heightened custody, audit, and access separation.
- **Community reserve:** locally held stock protected against unilateral regional or Starfleet seizure except under a reviewable emergency standard.

## Governance questions

A durable seed compact should address who owns seed, who may reproduce it, what data follows a sample, who verifies purity, how adverse results are disclosed, what emergency seizure permits, how decisions may be appealed, what obligations attach to Starfleet-supplied stock, and when emergency powers expire.

## Custody patterns

### Central Food Council custody

Efficient and auditable, but vulnerable to monopoly, political favoritism, identity correlation, and emergency powers without meaningful appeal.

### Starfleet trusteeship

Provides technical security and external review, but risks making independence dependent on a starship or distant command.

### Local sovereign custody

Protects autonomy and ecological knowledge, but creates uneven testing, trade barriers, and inconsistent containment.

### Cooperative or partitioned custody

Separates physical stock, genomic data, access keys, and audit authority among multiple institutions. This reduces unilateral control while increasing coordination and continuity requirements.

### Public commons

Maximizes access and innovation, but can expose dangerous control data, weaken consent, and make provenance difficult during crisis.

## Hearing and appeal

Allocation, quarantine, destruction, seizure, and exclusion decisions should identify a decision-maker, evidence standard, duration, review date, appeal path, and maintenance responsibility. Emergency action may precede a hearing when life is at risk, but the absence of later review is a campaign consequence, not neutral procedure.

## Ration identity

Ration records may be necessary for distribution but should not become automatic proof of citizenship, loyalty, medical status, or K-17 exposure. The marker crisis makes data linkage itself a command and civil-rights question.
