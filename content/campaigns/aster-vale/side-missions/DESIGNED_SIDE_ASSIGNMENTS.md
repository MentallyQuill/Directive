# Designed Side Assignments and Standing Work

Side work is part of the same campaign continuity. It inherits current routes, factions, ship condition, passenger load, crew state, known facts, and promises. Several assignments are intentionally calm and may remain unrelated to Halden, Far Lantern, Black Ledger, or the Protocol.

## The Schoolship

**ID:** `side-the-schoolship`  

**Kind:** side  

**Typical duration:** 1-2 sessions  

**Dramatic question:** Can schooling remain ordinary when every safe route is also a political statement?

Keep this assignment about ordinary life, education, risk, and route policy. It may remain calm unless existing world state causally introduces danger.

**Availability:** All of: quest prelude-the-blank-route is resolved

**Anchors:** locations halloway-colony, nysas-lantern, varins-rest; actors councilor-eme-saar, governor-lise-talar, sima-taren; factions nysas-lantern-council, ilyran-colonial-council. 

**Objectives**

- Establish a safe, repeatable school route.
- Set what passenger and location data the service must disclose.

**Pressures**

- Missed cycles separate children from families for weeks.
- A complete passenger list would expose multiple communities.

**Revelations and clue resilience**

- Students help maintain a route-marker chain as part of navigation instruction. [alternate routes allowed]

**Illustrative approaches, not limits**

- Escort one cycle
- Create an anonymous timetable
- Move classes to rotating sites
- Negotiate a protected educational corridor

**Authored outcome families**

- **A protected school corridor and privacy practice are established.** (`schoolship.protected`): regional-legitimacy +1; grant asset asset.schoolship-network (Schoolship Network).
- **The route is suspended and families improvise local instruction.** (`schoolship.suspended`): refugee-pressure +1; set schoolship-suspended = True.

**Delegation:** Permitted after at least 12 hour(s). Required assets: none. Risk: Delegation may produce a safe route but cannot establish broad disclosure policy without player review.

**Calm-content protection:** This assignment may remain ordinary, humane, technical, or personal. Do not force it into the Protocol conspiracy.
## Customs Without a Port

**ID:** `side-customs-without-a-port`  

**Kind:** side  

**Typical duration:** 1-2 sessions  

**Dramatic question:** Can a mobile institution be accountable without becoming easy to seize?

The problem is procedural legitimacy, corruption, and commerce rather than a hidden conspiracy. Existing Black Ledger pressure may complicate it but should not replace the customs issue.

**Availability:** Any of: quest chapter-1-the-missing-colony is resolved; locationKnown (locationId=sable-crossing)

**Anchors:** locations sable-crossing; actors magistrate-rina-esh, nyra-voss, lyra-chen; factions sable-civic-assembly, black-ledger-syndicate. 

**Objectives**

- Audit the rotating customs system.
- Create a transparent but survivable inspection process.

**Pressures**

- Each reform slows essential cargo.
- Some inspectors sell inspection priority or overlook coerced passengers.

**Revelations and clue resilience**

- The rotating system was designed to prevent any faction from capturing customs permanently. [alternate routes allowed]

**Illustrative approaches, not limits**

- Observe inspections
- Provide Starfleet technical support
- Create rotating civilian oversight
- License multiple competing inspection teams

**Authored outcome families**

- **Customs gains auditable rotation and appeal.** (`customs.reformed`): regional-legitimacy +1; criminal-exploitation -1; grant asset asset.sable-customs (Sable Customs Accord).
- **Customs fragments and private inspections proliferate.** (`customs.collapses`): criminal-exploitation +1; regional-legitimacy -1.

**Delegation:** Permitted after at least 10 hour(s). Required assets: none. Risk: Delegated reform may resolve procedures but not coercive detention disputes.

**Calm-content protection:** This assignment may remain ordinary, humane, technical, or personal. Do not force it into the Protocol conspiracy.

## The Quiet Census

**ID:** `side-the-quiet-census`  

**Kind:** side  

**Typical duration:** 1-2 sessions  

**Dramatic question:** How can a community count what it must sustain without making every person legible to power?

Make consent, aggregate planning, family linkage, and security-screening data separable. A census can succeed without creating one complete identity database.

**Availability:** Any of: quest chapter-4-routes-without-records is resolved; refugee-pressure gte 5

**Anchors:** locations nysas-lantern; actors councilor-eme-saar, tavra-nesh, ilan-korev; factions nysas-lantern-council. 

**Objectives**

- Produce reliable capacity data.
- Protect identity and family information through a consent model.

**Pressures**

- Without counts, food and atmosphere allocation fail.
- Residents remember lists used for military detention.

**Revelations and clue resilience**

- Most life-support decisions require household and cohort totals, not individual identity records. [alternate routes allowed]

**Illustrative approaches, not limits**

- Use anonymous household tokens
- Create a local encrypted registry
- Record only aggregate needs
- Allow separate voluntary family-reunification records

**Authored outcome families**

- **Nysa gains useful planning data without one exportable identity list.** (`census.trusted`): refugee-pressure -1; regional-legitimacy +1; grant asset asset.quiet-census (Quiet Census).
- **The census fails and rationing remains approximate.** (`census.refused`): clock.nysa-capacity +1; set quiet-census-refused = True.

**Delegation:** Permitted after at least 12 hour(s). Required assets: none. Risk: Delegation succeeds only with local consent and medical privacy review.

**Calm-content protection:** This assignment may remain ordinary, humane, technical, or personal. Do not force it into the Protocol conspiracy.

## Maps for Smugglers

**ID:** `side-maps-for-smugglers`  

**Kind:** side  

**Typical duration:** 1-2 sessions  

**Dramatic question:** Can a useful route remain lawful when the person who knows it is not?

Do not collapse smuggling, humanitarian transport, and coercion into one category. The player may prosecute one conduct while protecting another route function.

**Availability:** Any of: quest chapter-4-routes-without-records is resolved; quest chapter-5-the-price-of-passage is resolved

**Anchors:** locations sable-crossing, gravesend-veil; actors lio-marek, nyra-voss, magistrate-rina-esh, neral-thzor; factions free-passage-network, black-ledger-syndicate, sable-civic-assembly. 

**Objectives**

- Secure safe clinic access.
- Adjudicate the pilot's alleged conduct without treating route knowledge as guilt.

**Pressures**

- The same logs prove clinic deliveries and restricted-goods runs.
- Detaining the pilot may close the only proven medical route.

**Revelations and clue resilience**

- The pilot carried medicine, refugees, luxury contraband, and one coerced passenger on different runs. [alternate routes allowed]

**Illustrative approaches, not limits**

- Immunity for route testimony
- Limited prosecution with licensed replacement pilots
- Civil restitution and monitoring
- Seize the route data and prosecute fully

**Authored outcome families**

- **Route access is preserved while specific harmful conduct receives a lawful response.** (`maps.separated-liability`): regional-legitimacy +1; criminal-exploitation -1; grant asset asset.clinic-route (Clinic Route).
- **The pilot is detained or flees and the clinic route becomes unreliable.** (`maps.route-lost`): refugee-pressure +1; chart-restoration -1.

**Delegation:** Permitted after at least 12 hour(s). Required assets: none. Risk: Delegation may secure clinic access but immunity or prosecution terms require command review.
## The Hadran Funeral

**ID:** `side-the-hadran-funeral`  

**Kind:** side  

**Typical duration:** 1-2 sessions  

**Dramatic question:** Can enemies carry their dead across a border without claiming the living?

The funeral may be tense but should not automatically be a trap. Its value lies in ritual, disputed identity, family access, and whether temporary passage can occur without establishing a broader enforcement precedent.

**Availability:** Any of: quest chapter-2-haldens-shuttle is resolved; civil-war-spillover gte 4

**Anchors:** locations hadran-perimeter, varins-rest, sable-crossing; actors colonel-varek-sorn, prior-ael-varin, mara-dey; factions hadran-transitional-authority, free-passage-network. 

**Objectives**

- Establish respectful identification and family access.
- Define a limited passage and weapons protocol.

**Pressures**

- Hadran law classifies some dead as traitors whose families may be punished.
- Both sides fear that temporary passage will be cited as general inspection authority.

**Revelations and clue resilience**

- The accident mixed loyalist, deserter, and civilian remains beyond clean political classification. [alternate routes allowed]

**Illustrative approaches, not limits**

- Neutral ceremony at Varin's Rest
- Separate repatriation by family choice
- Starfleet custody and memorial
- Limited Hadran escort under disarmament

**Authored outcome families**

- **A neutral funeral creates a narrow humanitarian protocol.** (`funeral.shared-rite`): civil-war-spillover -1; regional-legitimacy +1; grant asset asset.humanitarian-passage (Humanitarian Passage Protocol).
- **The rite fails or separates by force, worsening the border posture.** (`funeral.breakdown`): civil-war-spillover +1; advance front.hadran-incursion by 1 stage(s).

**Delegation:** Permitted after at least 10 hour(s). Required assets: none. Risk: Delegation works if rules of engagement and custody are already set.

**Calm-content protection:** This assignment may remain ordinary, humane, technical, or personal. Do not force it into the Protocol conspiracy.
## The Missing Sensor Pod

**ID:** `side-the-missing-sensor-pod`  

**Kind:** side  

**Typical duration:** 1-2 sessions  

**Dramatic question:** How much should command risk to recover a machine that remembers where everyone went?

The pod is technically valuable and politically dangerous. Recovery, destruction, misinformation, and admission of loss are all viable depending on who has it and what has already been exposed.

**Availability:** Any of: quest chapter-4-routes-without-records is resolved; flag opening-doctrine-transparent = True

**Anchors:** locations gravesend-veil, kestrel-system, hadran-perimeter; actors omar-venn, sima-taren, major-kael-renn; factions starfleet-border-service, hadran-transitional-authority. 

**Objectives**

- Locate the sensor pod or establish who recovered it.
- Protect route and intelligence data.
- Restore mission-pod capability or accept the loss.

**Pressures**

- The pod contains both civilian route data and military sensor traces.
- Without it the Aster Vale loses high-resolution survey capacity.

**Revelations and clue resilience**

- A remote wipe protected some civilian data but left enough telemetry to identify the crossing family. [alternate routes allowed]

**Illustrative approaches, not limits**

- Recover by shuttle
- Remote-destruct the pod
- Trade for return
- Publish a false calibration notice
- Accept loss and protect exposed people

**Authored outcome families**

- **The pod returns with an auditable data review.** (`pod.recovered`): chart-restoration +1; crew-trust +1; grant asset asset.sensor-pod-restored (Restored Sensor Pod).
- **The pod is destroyed to protect data.** (`pod.destroyed`): chart-restoration -1; set sensor-pod-destroyed = True.
- **Another actor copies the data before recovery.** (`pod.compromised`): civil-war-spillover +1; advance front.chart-exposure by 1 stage(s); set sensor-pod-compromised = True.

**Delegation:** Permitted after at least 8 hour(s). Required assets: none. Risk: Delegation may recover hardware, but data-disclosure decisions require command.

## Route Review

**ID:** `standing-route-review`  

**Kind:** emergent  

**Typical duration:** variable  

**Dramatic question:** When should a route be opened, closed, restricted, forgotten, or placed in another custodian's hands?

This is the recurring mechanism for changing route status and custody without forcing every change into a main chapter. Require a causal trigger and record who gains or loses access.

**Availability:** All of: quest prelude-the-blank-route is resolved

**Anchors:** locations meridian-beacon, gravesend-veil, sable-crossing, nysas-lantern, far-lantern; actors sima-taren, ilan-korev, lyra-chen; factions starfleet-border-service, free-passage-network, lacuna-protocol-network. 

**Objectives**

- Gather current route and impact evidence.
- Set status, confidence, visibility, and custodian.
- Communicate the decision to affected actors.

**Pressures**

- A route change never affects every group equally.
- The world may change before the review is complete.

**Revelations and clue resilience**

- Route status is a distribution of access and risk, not merely a technical confidence label. [alternate routes allowed]

**Illustrative approaches, not limits**

- Open publicly
- Open conditionally
- Restrict to protected uses
- Close temporarily
- Leave undocumented under local custody

**Authored outcome families**

- **A route decision is committed with visible consequences.** (`review.committed`): chart-restoration +1.
- **The route remains conditional while pressure continues.** (`review.deferred`): advance front.chart-exposure by 1 stage(s).

**Delegation:** Permitted after at least 8 hour(s). Required assets: none. Risk: Technical review can be delegated; custody and access policy require player approval.
