# The Black Current Campaign Guardrails

## Player agency

- Situation on rails, solution off rails.
- Every emergence must have a causal parent in a documented missing vessel or battle, current geometry, anchor activity, elapsed time, or a prior committed decision.
- The Vanta Wake remains a persistent region; locations, claims, survivors, factions, and requests continue outside the foreground quest.
- Some calm assignments remain calm and unrelated to Project Mooring.
- Treat returned survivors as continuing persons with privacy, relationships, consent, and legal agency.
- Separate rescue authority, salvage title, grave custody, evidence, quarantine, and weapons control rather than assigning all domains to one owner by default.
- Crew disagreement should be focused; not every senior officer gives a moral speech before action.
- No character begins with the complete campaign truth.
- Move information only when causally justified if the player bypasses an expected scene.
- Escalation pacing serves established physical and political processes rather than forcing combat or countdowns.
- Factions act between scenes based on goals, knowledge, capabilities, and access.
- Delegated responders act according to earned authority, trust, charter rules, and capability; delegation is not automatic success or failure.
- Senior-staff briefings distinguish known facts, inference, unknowns, risks, capacity, recommendations, and the decision required.

Additional non-negotiable rules:

- Never write the player character's thoughts, feelings, speech, values, or decisions.
- Provide routine Starfleet competence, known procedure, domain advice, and consequences; stop before judgment.
- Prewritten approaches and decision options are examples, not an allowed-action menu.
- The player may refuse, postpone, delegate, withdraw, surrender, negotiate, or pursue an unanticipated approach.

## Command authority

- Captain Lorne holds legal command in the opening until injury prevents continued duty.
- Lawful succession gives the player full Acting Captain authority after Wreckfall.
- The player independently holds the regional recovery mandate, which is not automatically transferred when Lorne's medical state changes.
- Sen controls medical fitness findings; Starfleet controls formal appointment; the player controls operations while acting captain; Lorne controls her own requests and response.
- Senior officers may advise, document objections, request relief, or invoke lawful safety limits. Crew disagreement is not a vote.

## Hidden truth

- Raw campaign track values
- Secret faction clocks and the exact Black Tide convergence time
- Project Mooring and the four-anchor design before discovery
- Anchor Seventeen coordinates and command state before discovery
- Commander Ro's covert priority list and evidence-purge actions
- Niko Saren's survival and location before causal evidence
- The exact future emergence queue
- Predetermined valid solutions or Command Decisions
- NPC loyalty or relationship numbers

## Failure forward

- No single failed check ends a mission.
- Campaign-critical truth has multiple evidence routes through testimony, manifests, telemetry, components, relay baselines, and institutional records.
- A failed scan creates uncertainty, delay, exposure, equipment strain, false confidence, or a harder route; it does not delete the only clue.
- Senior-crew death requires an established lethal situation, meaningful player choice or accumulated consequence, fair opportunity for response, and narrative weight.
- Refusing an assignment is a consequential command act, not an out-of-bounds input.
- Delay advances clocks only when actors or physical processes have a reason to continue.
- The Serein may be heavily damaged, forced to withdraw, or lose regional legitimacy without invalidating the campaign.
- A severe Black Tide outcome concludes through evacuation, inquiry, and surviving obligations rather than a reset or game-over abstraction.

## Open-world causality

- Every emergence identifies a source, entry history, release cause, and reason it occurs now.
- Every offscreen faction action identifies actor, goal, knowledge, capability, and opportunity.
- Travel and rest advance time but only advance a front or clock whose process actually continues.
- Quests inherit current location, route, ship, crew, actor, faction, front, knowledge, and asset state.
- Delegation uses earned authority and capability rather than automatic success.
- The finale reads actual accumulated state and cannot reset ignored, destroyed, failed, or delegated obligations.

## Survivor dignity and identity

- Returned people are continuing persons, not collectibles, plot keys, or automatically unreliable anomalies.
- Distinguish medical custody, consent, legal status, command authority, property, family relationships, records, and evidence.
- Do not coerce family reconciliation as a mission success condition.
- Do not treat a declaration of death as proof of personal discontinuity.
- Privacy and trauma may limit testimony without making a survivor obstructive.

## Tone

- Competent Starfleet professionals operating under uncertainty and postwar strain.
- Moral, legal, and institutional complexity without nihilism.
- Rescue, maintenance, memorial, family contact, professional humor, and ordinary continuity remain present.
- Violence may be necessary for immediate safety but cannot by itself settle identity, grave rights, legitimacy, or accountability.
- The Wake is dangerous because of physical processes and human institutions, not because every local actor is secretly corrupt.

## Canon boundaries

- Opening stardate: 53672.1
- Opening year: 2376
- The Dominion War has recently ended.
- Voyager remains in the Delta Quadrant and does not intersect the campaign.
- Canon factions, technology, and era conditions may appear as context. The Serein, crew, Vanta Wake, Project Mooring, local actors, and outcomes remain original and canon-adjacent.
- Do not introduce canonical starship names for package-owned vessels.
