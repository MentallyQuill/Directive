# Vanta Wake Emergence and Recovery System

## Purpose

This authoring reference turns the campaign's central premise into repeatable open-world procedure. It is Director guidance, not a random table that overrides state.

## Creating an emergence

An emergence may enter the queue only when at least one causal source is established:

- a named missing ship, convoy, shuttle, weapons cluster, or battle record;
- forecast geometry from Ternion or a local pilot network;
- an Anchor Seventeen pulse or repair attempt;
- an advancing Current Convergence or Ordnance Drift front;
- a prior tow, destruction, route closure, or component removal that redirects stress;
- elapsed time attached to a documented release window.

Record confidence separately for source identity, timing, location, occupants, and hazard. Uncertainty is playable information.

## Emergence profile

Every profile should answer:

- What is returning?
- Who may be alive?
- What experienced duration do they report?
- What is physically failing now?
- What claims already exist?
- Which records or evidence are aboard?
- What ordnance or classified systems are present?
- Which factions know or suspect the event?
- Which responders can reach it before the Serein?
- What changes if no one acts?

## Response phases

1. **Forecast:** receive, verify, conceal, publish, or dispute a prediction.
2. **Arrival:** establish traffic control, responder boundaries, challenge procedures, and rescue doctrine.
3. **Stabilization:** arrest collision, reactor, atmosphere, weapons, and current hazards.
4. **Life search:** locate living persons without assuming records are accurate.
5. **Custody split:** assign temporary control of patients, remains, weapons, logs, property, and evidence by domain.
6. **Transfer:** tow, evacuate, quarantine, memorialize, destroy, or leave in controlled orbit.
7. **Aftermath:** create persistent claims, hearings, repairs, family contact, medical care, evidence chains, and follow-up threads.

The phases are functions, not mandatory scenes. The player may reorder or bypass them through a credible plan.

## Recovery capacity

Track at least:

- one major Serein tow slot;
- two heavy recovery shuttles;
- transporter cycling and pattern-buffer strain;
- modular medical, quarantine, morgue, and housing spaces;
- workbee and damage-control availability;
- allied tug and berth assets;
- current forecast confidence;
- active rescue sites and delegated commanders.

A capability committed to one site is unavailable elsewhere until released or a causal workaround is established.

## Custody domains

Do not use a single 'owner' field for everything aboard a wreck. Distinguish:

- living-person consent and medical custody;
- remains and grave custody;
- salvage title and repair labor;
- ship command and registry;
- personal property;
- weapons safety;
- classified technology;
- criminal or war-crime evidence;
- family records and cultural archives;
- navigation telemetry and Project Mooring evidence.

Temporary emergency custody is not permanent title. Rescue does not automatically confer ownership. Classification does not automatically defeat medical or legal rights.

## Failure transformations

Use these instead of hard stops:

- exact emergence time becomes a broader window;
- a safe route requires a pilot or slower approach;
- a scan yields false confidence that must be reconciled;
- the ship arrives after another faction establishes custody;
- a wreck breaks into several persistent objects;
- survivors are recovered but evidence or property disperses;
- ordnance is destroyed but the mechanism remains unknown;
- a witness survives but refuses public testimony;
- a tow succeeds at the price of Serein technical debt;
- a delegated ally succeeds under rules the player may dislike.

## Calm use

Not every profile requires catastrophe. A sealed lifeboat can hold letters rather than people. A wreck may contain no secret, only a difficult identification. A forecast may be used for a controlled science run. A tug crew may need maintenance and leave safely. Ordinary continuity prevents the Wake from becoming an escalating slot machine.
