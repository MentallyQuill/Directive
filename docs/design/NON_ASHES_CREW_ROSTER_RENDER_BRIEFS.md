# Non-Ashes Crew Roster Render Briefs

These briefs cover bundled hero-ship crews added after Ashes of Peace:

- U.S.S. Serein, Black Current
- U.S.S. Glass Harbor, Drowned Constellation
- U.S.S. Aster Vale, Unseen Border
- U.S.S. Eudora Vale, Broken Accord
- U.S.S. Celandine, Enemy's Garden

Ashes of Peace and the U.S.S. Breckenridge are intentionally excluded.

The package files define the crew names, ranks, billets, species, roles, and summaries. The facial details, grooming, scars, freckles, props, and portrait backgrounds below are production art direction for roster renders, not hidden campaign facts.

## Global Rendering Conventions

- Portrait framing: waist-up or chest-up roster portraits with the face clearly readable at small card sizes.
- Uniform baseline: late-24th-century Starfleet duty uniforms, clean but lived-in. Keep campaign-specific wear subtle unless the brief calls for crisis damage.
- Division colors:
  - Command and flight control: command red.
  - Operations, engineering, tactical, security, deck, and logistics: operations gold.
  - Science, medical, counseling, and public-health roles: science blue.
- Rank-pip convention:
  - Captain: 4 gold pips.
  - Commander: 3 gold pips.
  - Lieutenant Commander: 3 visible pips, 2 gold and 1 black.
  - Lieutenant: 2 gold pips.
  - Chief Petty Officer: 0 officer pips; use an enlisted chief insignia or rate marker instead.
- Lighting: readable professional light, not glamour lighting. Backgrounds should identify the officer's normal work environment without distracting from the face.
